About Us

Built by the KZ community

KZplus is a community-driven leaderboard platform dedicated to Kreedz — the movement-based climbing discipline in Counter-Strike 2. We track player records, map completions, and rankings across hundreds of maps and tiers.

KZ originated in the early days of Counter-Strike as a community mod where players race to reach the end of obstacle course maps using only precise movement — no weapons, no combat. Today it has evolved into one of CS2's most dedicated communities with thousands of active players worldwide.

Our goal is to make KZ stats accessible to everyone — whether you're a beginner discovering your first T1 map or an elite runner chasing world records on T7+ maps.
